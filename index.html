AAAI Group

Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) group is interested in research and knowledge dissemination of advanced analytics techniques as well as artificial intelligence. Advanced analytics techniques majorly include operations research and optimization. The AAAI group actively seeks to improve business operations.

AAAI lab

Apart from printer and a few desktops, funds from the above sponsored projects are utilized to develop a computational server facility. The server has 192 GB RAM and 48 cores with Intel(R) Xeon(R) Gold 6248R CPU @ 3.00GHz.

This server is available to students and faculty for their research and other academic activities.
