About AAAI Lab

The Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) Lab is a dynamic research group dedicated to pioneering advancements in data-driven decision-making, large scale optimization, and AI. We are interested in theoretical research as well as real-world applications in transportation and supply chains in the following areas.

Research Focus
  • ✓ Advanced Analytics: Applied Probability, large scale optimization, and queuing systems.
  • ✓ Artificial Intelligence: Machine learning, game theory, and reinforcement learning.
