About AAAI Lab

The Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) Lab is a dynamic research group dedicated to pioneering advancements in data-driven decision-making, optimization, and AI technologies. We are interested in theoretical research as well as real-world applications in transportation and supply chains.

Research Focus
  • ✓ Advanced Analytics: Applied Probability, optimization models, and queuing systems.
  • ✓ Artificial Intelligence: Machine learning, game theory, and reinforcement learning.
High-Performance Computing Infrastructure
Computational Server
  • CPU: Intel Xeon Gold 6248R (48 cores @ 3.00GHz)
  • Memory: 192GB DDR4 RAM

GPU-Accelerated System
  • GPUs: NVIDIA RTX A4000 (16GB VRAM)

Our computational resources are available to students and faculty for their research and other academic activities.
